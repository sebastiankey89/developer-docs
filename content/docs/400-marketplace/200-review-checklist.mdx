---
title: Review Checklist
excerpt: What you need to know before submitting your app.
---

Before submitting your app for review, make sure your app meets the following criteria:

1. Supports both 1080p and 720p resolutions.
2. Supports landscape (16:9) and portrait (9:16) screen orientations.
3. Tested performance on a real device.
4. Helpful error messaging is provided for invalid input values.
5. Has a unique icon and thumbnail.
6. Supports offline (if applicable).
7. High-resolution videos are scaled down to a max of 1080p (if applicable).
